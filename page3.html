Scottish Enterprise will support you to employ a temporary specialist to develop new markets in the South of England by:

  • paying 30% of your salary costs, up to a maximum of £18,000 for one year, excluding expenses, bonuses, benefits and employer’s NI contributions.

Your Responsibilities
  1. Based on the start date you've told us of 1st Jan 2017, we expect you to make four claims as follows:
    • April 2017 - £4,500
    • July 2017 - £4,500
    • October 2017 - £4,500
    • January 2018 - £4,500
  2. With each claim, you must provide evidence of spend as follows:
    • Copies of all salary slips for the time period of the claim
    • If you use BACS, a copy of your BACS run for the period, clearly identifying the individual salary being claimed
    • Bank statements that show either individual salary payments or the total for the BACS run for the period.
  3. You will notify us of any changes to claim dates or amounts.
